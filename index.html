About

Over three decades of expertise. A pioneer in digital dentistry.

AG Dental Lab was founded in 1994 by Abdullatif Ghatasheh, helping dentists deliver predictable, aesthetic, and reliable restorations since 1994. The lab carries his initials — and his standard — into every case today.

1994

Founded in Shmeisani

Established as Khalil Al-Rahman Dental Laboratory, built on precision from day one.

2006

First digital zirconia in Jordan

The first lab in the country to manufacture zirconia restorations digitally via CAD/CAM, using the DeguDent system.

2024

A new chapter — AG Dental Lab

Rebranded and established as an independent laboratory in Al-Rabiah, Amman.

Today

Trusted by 150+ dentists

More than 15,000 cases and 100,000 units delivered — and counting.

Mission

Accurate, reliable, innovative solutions — backed by world-class quality standards.

We deliver an integrated system of digital dental services — restorations, zirconia milling, precision metal fabrication, 3D printing, and technical support — to enhance the success of dental practices and raise patient satisfaction across the region.
